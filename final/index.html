Welcome to Barriers Across Borders!

We recognize that finding career-related opportunities for international students in United States is a significant challenge for many international students.

Our hope is to provide international students in the United States, particularly the students at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), a space to share their stories.
Our wish is that these community stories will provide some comfort and/or advice as they navigate through the barriers in place for students from outside of the US when pursuing career-related opportunities.

Hover over a state on the map to preview the number of testimonies from UCLA international students who have had opportunities in that state.

Click on a state on the map to look at stories from UCLA international students who have had opportunities in that state...
...or click on the survey button to share your own story.
